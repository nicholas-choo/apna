Foreword by Past President of APNA

Dr. Kenneth Kang

It is my sincere pleasure to welcome you to this website.


APNA is established to provide an oversight of the field of neurofeedback and biofeedback so as to promote and expand it as well as to safeguard consumer interests.


I would like to express my deepest gratitude for the practitioners and researchers who have come together to help making the establishment of APNA possible. With that, I also want to extend my warmest invitation to anyone who are passionate about this field to come join us and grow this field hand in hand with the community for the benefit of mankind.
