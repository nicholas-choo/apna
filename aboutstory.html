Our Vision

At APNA, our vision is to deepen our scientific understanding of Asian mindfulness and meditation techniques, exploring their profound health benefits. We are dedicated to promoting the application of these practices in society, with the aim of enhancing health, performance, and overall quality of life for individuals worldwide.

Our Mission

APNA's missions encompass promoting research collaboration between researchers, clinicians, and the community, advancing the professional clinical use of neurofeedback and biofeedback in the Asia Pacific region, and raising awareness among the general public about the transformative benefits of these practices. Together, we strive to foster a collaborative environment, elevate clinical standards, and empower individuals with the knowledge and potential of neurofeedback and biofeedback for improved well-being.
