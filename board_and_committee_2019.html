Vice President

Assoc Prof Dr. Nidal Kamel

EE Engineering, UTP

Dr. Nidal Kamel is a telecommunication specialist with expertise in statistical signal processing. He received his master and PhD degrees from the Technical University of Gdansk, Poland, in 1989 and 1994, respectively. Since 1994, he has been involved in various research projects related to statistical signal/image processing, including brain neuroimaging and video tensors processing.

He held an associate professor position at the Technical University of PETRONAS (UTP), Malaysia and played an instrumental role in establishing the Neuro Signal Processing Group at the national Center of Intelligent Signal and Image Research (CISIR). Dr. Kamel's research work focused on brain disorder assessment using different neuroimaging modalities. Dr. Panu Khuwuthyakorn

Saunprung Psychiatric Hospital

Dr. Khuwuthyakorn is a well-respected medical doctor in Chiang Mai. He graduated from Chiang Mai University and is board certified in psychiatry and family medicine. He previously worked at the National Psychiatric Institute, where he focused on mental health and addiction medicine. Currently he is a resident psychiatrist at Chiang Mai Psychiatric Hospital, a medical consultant at the Chiang Mai Methadone Clinic, a medical consultant at the Harm Reduction Centre and the Asian Mental Health Crisis Centre, and Chair of the Suicide Prevention Committee. He is certified in Addiction Medicine and is the leading medical doctor in Chiang Mai specialising in mental health, addiction and dual diagnosis.

Dr. Khuwuthyakorn is very experienced at working with mental health issues and addiction. He has treated hundreds of dual diagnosis patients who have co-occurring addiction and mental health issues. He oversees medication management and drug and alcohol detox, including methadone detox. He also works with specific mental health issues, including trauma related disorders, depression and anxiety.
