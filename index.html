Asia Pacific Neuro-biofeedback Association (APNA)

Welcome to the Asia Pacific Neuro-biofeedback Association (APNA), a non-profit membership organization for clinicians and researchers who are involved in the scientific research and clinical applications of neurofeedback, neurotherapy and biofeedback.

Learn more

Empowering Minds, Transforming Lives: Our Commitment at APNA

APNA aims to:

- Promote clinical and research applications in the field of neurofeedback and biofeedback

- Promote research collaboration between clinicians, researchers, institutions and the community to develop deeper understanding and applications

- Ensure professionalism - integrity, skills, knowledge, experience and ethical conduct

- Promote awareness of neurofeedback and biofeedback to the general public
