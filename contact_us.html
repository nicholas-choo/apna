Public Transport Directions:

From Bugis MRT Station, take Exit C. Exit from Bugis Junction Fountain side and walk along North Bridge Road towards the direction of National Library. Look out for Maybank opposite National Library. Cross the road to Maybank. Purvis Street will be on the left.


Our office is located in one of those red-framed windows shop houses. The lobby entrance to our office is 2 units away from Kiliney coffee shop. Enter the lobby and go to second floor.


Driving Directions:

Purvis street is opposite the National Library and Bras Basah Complex. As it is a one way street, drivers will have to enter via Beach Road and make a left turn into Purvis street.

Parking Options:

  • Purvis Street Carpark - Along Purvis Street.
  • Beach Centre Carpark - 15 Beach Road, S189677. When you first turn into Purvis Street, make another left turn and the carpark entrance is on the left.
  • North Bridge Centre Carpark - 420 North Bridge Road, S188727. Driving along Purvis Street, make a right turn (just before Maybank), the carpark entrance is on your left.
  • National Library Carpark - 100 Victoria Street, S188064.
